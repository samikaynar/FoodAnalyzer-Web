What is Know Your Food?

This is an AI-powered web application that provides instant health analysis of food products using advanced machine learning technology.

How It Works

Simply enter any food product name, and our AI system will analyze its nutritional value and provide a comprehensive health assessment including:

  • Health score from 1 to 10
  • Key health factors and ingredients
  • Potential benefits and risks
  • Healthier alternatives
  • Personalized recommendations

Our Technology

Powered by OpenAI's GPT-3.5 Turbo and built with Flask backend, our system provides accurate, real-time food analysis without requiring users to enter any API keys.

Our Mission

To empower people to make informed food choices through accessible AI technology and promote healthier eating habits.
